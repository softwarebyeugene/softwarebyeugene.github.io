Privacy Policy for Scoundrel

Last Updated: May 16, 2026

Scoundrel is published by SoftwareByEugene LLC. This policy explains what information the app uses and how it is handled.

Information We Collect

Scoundrel does not ask you to create an account and does not collect your name, email address, contacts, photos, precise location, or messages.

The app may collect limited app analytics to help us understand app usage and improve reliability. These analytics may include:

  • App lifecycle events, such as app launch, foreground, and background events.
  • Screen and paywall view events.
  • Purchase flow events, such as purchase started, completed, cancelled, failed, or restore used.
  • Basic app context, such as app version, build number, platform, and game mode.
  • A randomly generated app-specific identifier used to group analytics events for this app installation.

Analytics Provider

Scoundrel uses PostHog to process analytics events. Analytics are used only to understand app usage, diagnose issues, and improve the product. Learn more at PostHog's Privacy Policy.

Purchases

Scoundrel offers an optional non-consumable in-app purchase. Purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store your payment card information.

Advertising and Tracking

Scoundrel does not show third-party ads and does not use Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework to track you across other companies' apps or websites.

Data Stored on Your Device

Game progress, settings, purchase entitlement state, and the app-specific analytics identifier may be stored locally on your device or in the iOS Keychain so the app can function properly.

Children's Privacy

Scoundrel is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy if app functionality or legal requirements change. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date.

Contact
