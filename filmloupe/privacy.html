Film Loupe Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 13, 2026

Film Loupe is designed as a private, on-device utility.

Data collection

Film Loupe does not collect, track, sell, or share personal data. The app does not require an account and does not include advertising or third-party analytics.

Camera

Camera access is used only to show film negatives as positives live on your device. Camera frames are processed on device and are not transmitted by Film Loupe.

Photos

If you choose to save an image, Film Loupe requests permission to add the saved inverted view to your Photos library. The app does not need to read your photo library.

Contact

For privacy questions, email support@softwarebyeugene.com.
