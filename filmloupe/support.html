Film Loupe Support

Film Loupe is a simple iPhone and iPad utility for viewing photo negatives as positives live.

Help

  • Grant camera access to use the live negative viewer.
  • Drag vertically on the viewer to adjust contrast.
  • Drag vertically on the right edge to adjust screen brightness.
  • Tap the light button to use the device as an adjustable lightbox.
  • Tap the save button to save the current inverted view to Photos.
