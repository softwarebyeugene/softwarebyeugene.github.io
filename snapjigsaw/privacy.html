Privacy Policy for Snap Jigsaw

Effective Date: May 4, 2025

At SoftwareByEugene LLC, your privacy is our priority. Snap Jigsaw is designed to be fun, simple, and respectful of your personal information.

🔒 What We Don’t Collect

We believe in keeping things private. Snap Jigsaw does not collect or store:

  • Your name, email address, or contact information
  • Your location or device data
  • App usage stats or puzzle history
  • Any advertising or third-party tracking data

🕹 Game Center

If you choose to sign in with Apple’s Game Center, your scores and achievements may appear on Apple’s leaderboards. SoftwareByEugene LLC does not access, store, or share this data.

Learn more in Apple’s Privacy Policy.

🖼 Puzzle Artwork

Snap Jigsaw uses images from Pexels.com to bring you a beautiful variety of puzzles. While images are fetched from Pexels’ servers, no personal information is shared with them.

For more, see Pexels’ Privacy Policy.

🔌 No Other Connections

Snap Jigsaw doesn’t connect to any other external services. SoftwareByEugene LLC does not collect, store, or share any of your personal data—period.
