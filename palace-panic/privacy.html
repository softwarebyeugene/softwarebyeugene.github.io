Privacy Policy for Palace Panic - CardCraft

Last Updated: July 19, 2026

Palace Panic - CardCraft is published by SoftwareByEugene LLC. We designed the app to work without collecting personal data.

Data Collection

We do not collect, store, transmit to our servers, or sell your personal data.

The app does not use third-party analytics or advertising SDKs and does not collect your name, email address, precise location, contacts, photos, messages, or gameplay analytics.

Tracking and Advertising

Palace Panic - CardCraft contains no third-party advertising and does not track you across other companies' apps or websites.

Data Stored on Your Device

Settings, tutorial progress, solo-game state, visual preferences, and an optional nearby-player display name may be stored locally on your device so the app can function. This local data is not sent to SoftwareByEugene LLC.

Nearby Multiplayer

If you choose nearby multiplayer, the app uses Apple's local-network technologies to connect devices in the same area. Player display names and the game state needed for the current match are shared directly between participating devices. SoftwareByEugene LLC does not receive, collect, or store this information.

In-App Purchases

Palace Panic - CardCraft offers an optional one-time Premium purchase. Purchases and entitlement verification are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store your payment-card information.

Apple Services

Apple platform features, including in-app purchases and local-network permissions, are governed by Apple's terms and privacy practices.

Learn more at Apple Privacy Policy.

Children's Privacy

Because we do not collect personal data, Palace Panic - CardCraft does not knowingly collect personal information from children or adults.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy if app functionality or legal requirements change. Updates will be posted here with a revised Last Updated date.

Contact
