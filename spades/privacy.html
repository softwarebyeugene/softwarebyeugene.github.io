Privacy Policy for Spades - Cardcraft

Last Updated: July 16, 2026

Spades - Cardcraft is published by SoftwareByEugene LLC. We designed the app to be privacy-first and to work without collecting personal data.

Data Collection

We do not collect, store, transmit, or sell your personal data.

This includes your name, email address, precise location, contacts, photos, messages, and usage analytics.

Tracking and Advertising

Spades - Cardcraft does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or cross-app tracking technologies.

Data Stored on Your Device

Game-related information such as settings, progress, preferences, player names, and premium entitlement status may be stored locally on your device so the app can function properly. This local data stays on your device and is not sent to SoftwareByEugene LLC.

Nearby Multiplayer

If you choose to play nearby multiplayer, Spades - Cardcraft may use Apple's local network technologies to connect devices in the same area. Player display names and game state may be shared directly between participating nearby devices for the current game session. SoftwareByEugene LLC does not receive, collect, or store this information.

Apple Services

If you choose to use Apple platform features such as in-app purchases or local network permissions, those features are provided by Apple and are governed by Apple's terms and privacy practices.

Learn more at Apple Privacy Policy.

Children's Privacy

Because we do not collect personal data, Spades - Cardcraft does not knowingly collect personal information from children or adults.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy if app functionality or legal requirements change. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date.
