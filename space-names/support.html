Space Names Support

Space Names labels your current macOS Space in the menu bar so you can keep track of work contexts.

Contact

For help, bug reports, or feedback, email eugene@softwarebyeugene.com.

Troubleshooting

  • Use the menu bar item to rename or clear the current Space label.
  • Use ⌥⌘L to rename the current Space.
  • If Space detection is unavailable, Space Names will show a fallback status and labels may apply to a single fallback Space.
